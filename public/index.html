    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can this replace a dentist or doctor?

    No — absolutely not. AICanScanO is a screening aid designed to help flag images that may warrant further clinical evaluation. It does not replace professional clinical examination, biopsy, or any form of medical diagnosis. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.

    How accurate is this tool?

    AI models for oral lesion detection have shown high sensitivity and specificity in research studies (AUC 0.85–0.98 on curated datasets). However, real-world performance may differ due to image quality, lighting, device variation, and population differences. This tool has not been clinically validated for diagnostic use and may produce incorrect results on individual cases.

    What should I do if I'm worried about a lesion?

    If you notice any persistent sore, white or red patch, lump, or unusual change in your mouth lasting more than 2 weeks, seek professional care promptly — regardless of what any screening tool says. Visit a dentist, oral medicine specialist, or your primary care physician.

    Is my image stored or shared?

    Images are processed for analysis and are not permanently stored or shared with third parties in the current prototype. In a production deployment, strict data governance, encryption, and consent frameworks would apply. See our Privacy policy for details.

    Who is this tool for?

    AICanScanO is designed for dentists, primary care physicians, community health workers, and individuals who want an initial AI-assisted screening of intra-oral images. It is not a substitute for clinical examination and should be used as a supplementary tool only.

    ⚠️ Important Disclaimer: This tool is experimental and does not provide medical diagnoses. It is intended as an AI-assisted screening aid only. Always consult a qualified clinician for any concern about oral health.
